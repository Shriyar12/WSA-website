Our Services

Animal rescue

We rescue animals in distress, including those injured, abandoned, or subjected to cruelty.We provide immediate medical attention, shelter, and rehabilitation for rescued animals.Our team works tirelessly to nurse them back to health and prepare them for adoption or release, depending on the situation

Rehabilation Center

We provide essential veterinary services to ensure the health and well-being of animals in our care.Our team of qualified veterinarians performs routine check-ups, vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and medical treatments.We also offer emergency medical assistance and surgeries when required

Active Adoptions

Take home any of the shelter animals that are put up for adoption . You can get in touch with WSA via phone, email or any other social media .Walk into the centre any day between 10:00 a.m. to 5 p.m. to meet all the wet noses and wagging tails. Trust us, you'd have never seen so much happiness under one roof!

Passive Adoptions

You can help WSA by sponsoring the monthly stay, food and basic medicines of any of the rescued animals at a fee mentioned below:

  • Dogs - ₹1500 monthly and ₹50 per day
  • Cows and Calf - ₹2000 monthly and ₹66 per day
  • Goat and Sheep - ₹1000 monthly and ₹33 per day
  • Cats - ₹900 monthly and ₹30 per day
